About Us

Every year, roughly one-third of the food produced for human consumption in the world - approximately 1.3 billion tonnes - is lost or wasted. So why not try to save some food and give it to someone who might need it or have a better use for it? I created this website to give back to the community and so that we can live in a world where there is no hunger, no poverty, and where we are sustainable and responsible without wasting resources.
